Quenching the Curiosity Behind Prompt Engineering

I was curious what prompt engineering is all about. So I took a course that showed up on my feed. It also said it was free and gives a certificate. So why not? IBM provides a prompt engineering course through its cognitiveclass.ai platform. You can check it out here. Any one interested to learn about Prompt engineering can take this up.

I believe as AI is progressing and LLM is taking over the internet. I am sure everybody has something to take away from this course. I am listing out my take aways below:

  • Understanding what prompt engineering is.
  • Understanding a little about the background technology to execute it well.
  • Types of prompting techniques.
  • Naive prompting.
  • Persona prompting.
  • Interview prompting.
  • Chain of thought prompting.
  • Tree of thought prompting.
  • Nova system.

Out of these, I was really impressed by the Nova system. Imagine having the knowledge of an expert panel to help us reach our goals.
